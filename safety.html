Campus Safety Tips

  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Use the buddy system when walking at night
  • Know the locations of emergency phones on campus
  • Keep your dorm room locked at all times
  • Report suspicious activities to campus security
  • Attend campus safety orientations and workshops

Online Safety Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts
  • Be cautious about what personal information you share online
  • Update your devices and applications regularly
  • Be wary of phishing attempts and suspicious emails
  • Use two-factor authentication when available
  • Connect to secure Wi-Fi networks or use a VPN

Transportation Safety Tips

  • Use campus shuttle services when available
  • Share your location with trusted friends when traveling
  • Verify the identity of ride-share drivers before entering the vehicle
  • Have your keys ready before approaching your car
  • Avoid distractions like texting while walking
  • Use well-lit and populated routes, especially at night

  • Stay hydrated and be cautious with alcohol consumption
  • Learn basic self-defense techniques
  • Keep friends informed about your whereabouts
